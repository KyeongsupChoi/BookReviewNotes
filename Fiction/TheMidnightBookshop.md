## Thoughts and Opinions

This book was a terrific waste of time and every damn plot turn was predictable and hinted so heavily it was like it was being splashed with a bucket. I understand the longing of a magic solution to life's mundane problems and the power of books even fiction, but goddamn if this book didn't pat itself on the back every step of the way. The most egregious part was when the author had the magic character take credit for the accomplishments of great people in history and even the amazing book charity work Dolly Parton did for kids. The underlying message of optimism, autonomy, and self-improvement is great and I agree with them. The delivery this book attempted made me want to quit reading books.
